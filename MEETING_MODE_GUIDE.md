## How to Use During Your Meeting

### 1. **Enable Meeting Mode**
- In the sidebar, toggle **"Weekly Meeting Mode"** ON
- Dashboard shows only the last 7 days, grouped by relevance

### 2. **As You Discuss Each Item**

#### Check it off:
- Click the checkbox on the left when you've discussed an item
- The item will fade to show it's been covered
- Track your progress at the top (Discussed counter increases)

#### Flag for follow-up:
- Click **"üìå"** button on the right to flag items needing action
- Button changes to **"‚úì Follow-up"** when flagged
- Item appears in the Follow-up Summary at the bottom

#### Add action notes:
- When you flag an item, a **"üìù Add action notes"** section appears
- Click to expand and type:
  - What action is needed
  - Who owns it
  - Timeline
- Example: *"Policy team to draft brief | Sarah | This week"*

### 3. **Monitor Your Progress**
At the top you'll see:
- **High Priority Items** - Total items requiring attention
- **Discussed** - How many you've covered
- **Follow-up Flagged** - Items assigned for action

### 4. **Review Follow-up Summary**
At the bottom of the page:
- See all flagged items in one place
- Review action notes
- Remove items if needed (if accidentally flagged)

### 5. **Export Meeting Notes**
When done:
- Click **"üì• Export Notes"** to download a formatted text file
- Includes:
  - Meeting date
  - All follow-up items
  - Action assignments and owners
  - Links to articles
- Send this to the team after the meeting

### 6. **Start Fresh Next Week**
- Click **"üîÑ Reset Meeting Tracking"** to clear all checkboxes and flags
- Start your next meeting with a clean slate

## Action Note Format

**Good action notes:**
- "Brief policy team on FEOC impact | Sarah | This week"
- "Draft op-ed on permitting delays | Comms team | Pitch by Friday"
- "Schedule call with DOE contact | GR team | Next month"

**Bad action notes:**
- "Follow up" (too vague)
- "Important" (not actionable)
- "TBD" (defeats the purpose)

## Troubleshooting

**Q: My checkboxes aren't saving**
A: They're stored in the session - they reset when you refresh the browser. Export notes before closing!

**Q: I accidentally flagged an item**
A: Click the "‚úì Follow-up" button again OR use "Remove" in the Follow-up Summary

**Q: Can I edit notes after the meeting?**
A: Yes! As long as you haven't reset tracking or closed the browser, notes are editable

**Q: How do I clear everything for next week?**
A: Click "üîÑ Reset Meeting Tracking" - this clears all checkboxes, flags, and notes
